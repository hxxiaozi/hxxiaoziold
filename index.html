I am Xiaouxan Han(also know as xiaozi / 笑耔:), an artist currently studying at SAIC. Through my works, I express and explore the meaning of life and the world in which we live by using a variety of media. Art, design, media, and technology all play a significant role in my work, and are all interrelated rather than separated. My art is always a reflection of my feelings, my personality, my way of life, and my view of the world. Little things in life often give me inspiration for big thoughts.
This is a space where you can explore and have fun! Hope you enjoy!
